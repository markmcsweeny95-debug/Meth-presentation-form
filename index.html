What is Crystal Meth?

Crystal meth is a powerful stimulant drug that affects the brain and body. It increases dopamine levels, creating intense energy and euphoria, but is highly addictive.

Effects

Short-term effects include energy, alertness, and reduced appetite.

Long-term effects include addiction, paranoia, tooth decay, and brain damage.

Legal Status in the UK

Crystal meth is a Class A drug in the UK.

Possession can lead to up to 7 years in prison, and supplying can lead to life imprisonment.
