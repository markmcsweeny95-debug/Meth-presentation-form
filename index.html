Crystal Meth

An educational presentation about the effects, risks and dangers of methamphetamine.

What Is Crystal Meth?

Crystal methamphetamine is a powerful stimulant drug that affects the central nervous system. It can create intense feelings of energy and confidence but is highly addictive and dangerous.

Effects

  • Extreme energy
  • Paranoia and anxiety
  • Rapid heart rate
  • Memory problems
  • Tooth decay
  • Severe addiction
